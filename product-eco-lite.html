Product — Transit Living

Eco-Lite

Lightweight, relocatable bamboo living units designed for temporary and semi-permanent deployment — for events, remote worksites, glamping, and transit accommodation.

Enquire Now View Unit Types
Home›Products›Eco-Lite (Transit Living Units)

Eco-Lite units are prefabricated bamboo living spaces — fully assembled at our workshop and transported to your site ready to occupy. They combine the warmth of natural bamboo with the practical requirements of temporary or permanent lightweight accommodation.

The Concept

Living Lightly, Living Beautifully

Traditional temporary accommodation is often uninspiring and wasteful. The Eco-Lite reframes the concept — a bamboo unit that is thermally comfortable, visually striking, and gentle on the earth.

Designed to be relocatable, they can be moved between sites without dismantling — and at end of life, every component is biodegradable or recyclable.

  • Fully assembled off-site — delivered and placed by crane / forklift
  • Relocatable without dismantling
  • Natural bamboo interior — thermally insulating
  • Available with solar, water, and sanitation hookups
  • Can be deployed as standalone or in clusters
  • BIS-treated bamboo for longevity and pest resistance
