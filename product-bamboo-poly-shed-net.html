Bamboo Poly-Shed Net

Robust, eco-friendly shade net structures with bamboo framing — in panel-based and truss-based configurations spanning 12 ft to 36 ft.

Request a Quote Explore Types
Home›Products›Bamboo Poly-Shed Net

Our Bamboo Poly-Shed Net combines the structural strength of bamboo framing with high-quality shade netting — providing an eco-friendly, cost-effective alternative to steel-frame poly-sheds for horticulture, floriculture, and protected farming.

Type 1

Panel Based

Pre-fabricated bamboo panel modules assembled side-by-side to create the structure. Suitable for smaller spans and modular layouts.

Available Spans: 12 ft  ·  16 ft  ·  24 ft

Type 2

Truss Based

Pre-fabricated bamboo truss systems for larger spans. Provides clear internal space with no intermediate columns within the span.

Available Spans: 24 ft  ·  36 ft
