Portfolio — Hospitality

Conference cum Dining Hall

Nestled within the forest landscape of Pench Tiger Reserve, this conference cum dining pavilion was designed for Restfo Pench Tiger Resort — a hospitality property that needed a large gathering space that felt rooted in its jungle surroundings rather than imposed upon them.

The brief called for a structure that could accommodate both formal conferences and open-air dining, with an unobstructed internal span, natural materials, and a silhouette that complemented rather than competed with the forest canopy. Wonder Grass designed and delivered a full bamboo structural system — columns, beams, and a custom long-span truss roof — fabricated entirely off-site at the Nagpur workshop before being transported to Pench for assembly.

Building within a protected tiger reserve meant that on-site construction time, vehicle movements, and material waste had to be kept to an absolute minimum. All bamboo components were treated, dimensioned, numbered, and flat-packed in Nagpur so that the on-site team could assemble the structure rapidly and cleanly, with minimal disruption to the forest environment.

  • Custom long-span bamboo truss system for column-free interior
  • Full off-site prefabrication — minimal on-site fabrication work
  • Treated bamboo for durability in the humid forest microclimate
  • Assembly designed for restricted vehicle access within the reserve
  • Natural finish palette harmonising with jungle surroundings
