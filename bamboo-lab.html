Bamboo Lab

Where We Push Bamboo Further

The Bamboo Lab is our in-house research and development initiative — exploring new applications, developing new structural systems, and testing what bamboo is truly capable of.

Home›Bamboo Lab

At Wonder Grass, we don't just build with bamboo — we continue to study it, test it, and develop new ways to use it. The Bamboo Lab is where ideas that don't fit a standard product catalogue come to life.

What is the Bamboo Lab?

Research, Development & Collaborative Design

The Bamboo Lab is our ongoing initiative to expand what is possible with bamboo as a building material. It sits alongside our product range — not replacing it, but feeding it. Ideas that work in the lab eventually become new products, new systems, or new ways of building.

We also use the Bamboo Lab as a space for collaboration — with architects, engineers, designers, and institutions who want to explore bamboo-based solutions for specific challenges.

  • In-house structural system development
  • New application research across scales
  • Collaborative design with architects and engineers
  • Material testing and performance documentation
  • Prototype fabrication and field testing
Current Work

What We're Developing

A look at the ideas and systems currently being explored and developed within the Bamboo Lab.

Structural Systems

Bamboo Space Frame

An in-house developed structural system for large-span applications using bamboo in a three-dimensional frame configuration. Designed for spans not achievable with conventional bamboo truss systems.

Product Design

Outdoor Furniture Range

A casual outdoor furniture range developed for urban homes — chairs, tables, and loungers that bring the warmth and texture of bamboo into everyday living spaces. Designed for durability in Indian climates.
