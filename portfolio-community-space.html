Portfolio — Community

Community Space

Snehanchal is a social organisation based in Nagpur that works with children and communities in need. They required a dedicated gathering space — a hall that could host community meetings, children's programmes, cultural events, and workshops — built with materials that reflected their values of sustainability and simplicity.

Wonder Grass designed and built a bamboo community hall that is open, warm, and welcoming. The structure uses bamboo columns and beams with a bamboo-truss roof system, bamboo panel screens for the walls, and a natural material palette throughout. The design prioritises natural light and cross-ventilation, keeping the space comfortable through Nagpur's hot summers without relying on mechanical cooling.

As with all Wonder Grass projects, the components were prefabricated off-site at the Nagpur workshop and assembled on-site in a short window — minimising disruption to the organisation's daily activities and reducing construction waste. The result is a space that is genuinely used and loved by the community it was built for.

  • Bamboo column, beam and truss roof structural system
  • Bamboo panel screens — walls and partitions
  • Natural light and cross-ventilation design
  • Full off-site prefabrication for fast, clean assembly
  • Low-maintenance natural material finish throughout
