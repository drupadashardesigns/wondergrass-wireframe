Process & Approach

Designing for People, Building with Purpose

[ Describe the brief from Snehanchal: an affordable, durable, and welcoming space that could serve a diverse urban community in Nagpur. The challenges of designing within a budget while maintaining quality and structural integrity. ]

[ Wonder Grass response: the use of standardised bamboo building components to keep costs predictable, bamboo panels for quick partition walls, and a clear-span roof structure that maximises usable floor space without interior columns. ]

[ Sustainability angle: bamboo's low cost, fast renewability, and thermal comfort properties making it ideal for a community space in central India's warm climate. The long-term maintenance advantages passed on to the client. ]

  • Affordable construction using standardised bamboo components
  • Column-free clear-span interior for flexible use
  • Bamboo panel walls for rapid enclosure and natural aesthetics
  • Thermal comfort designed for Nagpur's warm climate
  • Low maintenance and long-term durability for community use
