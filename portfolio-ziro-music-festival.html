Portfolio — Events & Festivals

Ziro Music Festival

[ Description of the Ziro Music Festival project in Arunachal Pradesh. Background on the festival — one of India's most celebrated independent music festivals set in the stunning Ziro Valley, a UNESCO World Heritage nomination site. The scale of the festival, number of attendees, and the role bamboo structures play in creating the festival's distinctive atmosphere. ]

[ Detail of the structures designed and built: the main day stage, night stage, food court, VIP lounge, tourism pavilion, visitors plaza, and the Popi Sermin structure. How Wonder Grass designed structures that could be assembled efficiently on the remote festival site, with transport logistics from workshop to Ziro factored into the design. ]

[ The aesthetic dimension: bamboo structures that are visually dramatic against the backdrop of the Ziro Valley's rice fields and pine forests — and how the material speaks to the region's strong bamboo culture. ]

  • Flat-pack component system for remote transport to Arunachal Pradesh
  • Rapid on-site assembly within tight festival setup timelines
  • Wind-load engineered open-span stage and canopy structures
  • Culturally resonant material — bamboo native to the region
  • Multiple distinct structures across the festival grounds
