The Bamboo Farm Shed BIY Kit delivers everything needed to construct a sturdy bamboo farm shed — pre-cut, pre-treated, and numbered components with assembly instructions. No welding, no heavy machinery. Just simple tools and a small team.

What is a BIY Kit?

Build It Yourself — Designed to Be Assembled by Anyone

Our BIY (Build-It-Yourself) system takes the complexity out of bamboo construction. All structural elements are pre-cut, pre-drilled, and pre-treated at our workshop — then delivered to your site flat-packed with clear step-by-step assembly guides.

  • No specialist bamboo craftsmen required on-site
  • All components numbered and labelled for easy assembly
  • Can be erected by 3–4 labourers with basic hand tools
  • Pre-treated against pests, fungi, and moisture
  • Modular — add more bays to expand over time
  • Delivered to site in a single truck load (typical kit)

Assembly Guide

How to Assemble Your Kit

1

Prepare the Foundation

Mark out the footing positions, dig peg holes or set concrete footings as per the included foundation plan.

2

Erect the Columns

Set the labelled columns in the prepared footings. Plumb and temporarily brace.

3

Install Ridge & Beams

Lift and bolt the ridge beam and side beams into position using the pre-drilled holes and supplied hardware.

4

Fix Rafters & Purlins

Attach rafters at specified spacing, then lay purlins across — all pre-cut to length with pre-drilled fixing points.

5

Install Wall & Roof Cover

Fix wall panels or bracing as required, then lay your chosen roof covering per the included instructions.

6

Final Check & Handover

Check all fixings, remove temporary bracing, and your farm shed is ready for use.
