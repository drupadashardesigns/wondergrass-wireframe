B-POD Cottages

Compact, character-rich bamboo cottages for resorts, farmstays, eco-retreats, and glamping sites — in two footprint sizes.

Enquire Now View B-POD Types
Home›Products›B-POD Cottages

B-POD Cottages are prefabricated bamboo guest accommodation units — thoughtfully designed to deliver a rich, natural stay experience. Each B-POD is a standalone cottage with its own personality, fully finished and ready to host from day one.

The B-POD Philosophy

Tiny Footprint. Big Experience.

In an era of over-built hospitality, B-POD is a return to essentials — natural light, natural material, and a connection to the landscape. Compact enough to tuck into a forest, a hillside, or a garden, yet thoughtfully designed to offer genuine comfort.

B-PODs are prefabricated at our workshop, transported, and assembled on-site in days — not months. They can be deployed as a single unit or a cluster, and they perform well in rain, heat, and humidity across India's varied climates.

  • Prefabricated for quality control and speed of delivery
  • Thermally comfortable bamboo shell — insulating naturally
  • Finished internally with bamboo lining, flooring & joinery
  • Available with attached or separate en-suite bathroom
  • Deck / verandah option on all types
  • Climate-appropriate — designed for Indian conditions
  • Structurally certified bamboo frame
