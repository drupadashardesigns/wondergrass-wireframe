Portfolio — Outdoor Structures

Outdoor Structures

A collection of standalone outdoor bamboo structures — machaans, pergolas, and pavilions — built across various sites in India. Each piece is custom-designed for its site, client, and climate, workshop-fabricated at Nagpur, and assembled on location.

From elevated dining platforms inspired by the traditional forest machaan, to overhead pergola structures that define outdoor living spaces, Wonder Grass brings the same engineering rigour and material craft to outdoor structures as it does to its larger buildings.

  • Custom design for each site and client brief
  • Full off-site prefabrication for quality and speed
  • Treated bamboo engineered for outdoor durability
  • Steel-reinforced base connections for structural stability
  • Delivered and assembled anywhere across India
