Our Approach

Custom Designed, Precision Built

[ Describe the Wonder Grass approach to outdoor structures: every structure begins with an understanding of the site — the climate, soil, surrounding landscape, how people will move through and use the space, and what visual character the client wants to create. ]

[ The design process: sketches to CAD drawings, material specification, structural engineering review, and client sign-off before fabrication begins. All components are fabricated at the Wonder Grass workshop using seasoned, treated bamboo, then transported to site for assembly. ]

[ Durability and maintenance: how Wonder Grass treats bamboo for outdoor use — preservative treatment, UV-resistant finishes, steel base connections — to ensure structures that perform in Indian climates for years with minimal maintenance. ]

  • Site-specific custom design for every project
  • Full off-site prefabrication for quality control
  • Treated bamboo engineered for outdoor durability
  • Steel-reinforced base connections for structural stability
  • Delivered and assembled anywhere across India
